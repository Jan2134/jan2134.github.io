AuraEat

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

AuraEat is a circadian nutrition app that helps you align your eating window with your biology. We are committed to collecting the minimum data necessary to make the app work and never selling or sharing your personal information with third parties.

What we collect and why

Account identity

When you sign in with Apple, Apple provides us with a unique user identifier and, if you choose, an email address (which may be an Apple relay address). We store this solely to link your data to your account so you can restore it if you switch devices or reinstall the app.

Meal logs

When you manually log a meal, we store the grade you selected (A–F) and the timestamp. We do not store photos, AI analysis, or any description of what you ate.

Drink logs

When you log hydration, we store the drink type and amount in millilitres, and the timestamp.

Sleep, heart rate, and HRV

AuraEat reads Sleep Analysis, Heart Rate, and Heart Rate Variability from Apple Health to calculate your personalised eating window and Aura Score. This data never leaves your device. It is processed locally and is never transmitted to our servers.

What we do not collect

  • Photos or camera data
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Browsing or search history
  • Any data sold to or shared with third parties for advertising

How your data is stored

Data that lives only on your device — HealthKit signals, app settings, streaks — is stored using iOS encrypted secure storage and never leaves your phone.

Meal logs and drink logs are synced to our cloud database hosted by Supabase in the EU. Access is enforced at the database level so only your account can read or write your own records.

Third-party services

Service Purpose Data shared
Apple Sign In Authentication Apple user ID
Supabase Cloud database (EU-hosted) Meal logs, drink logs
Apple HealthKit Read biometrics locally Nothing — stays on device

Your rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Profile → Settings → Delete Account. Upon deletion, all records linked to your account are permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

If you are in the European Union, you have the right to access, correct, export, or erase your personal data at any time. To make a request, contact us at the address below.

Changes to this policy

If our data practices change in a material way, we will update this page and notify you inside the app before the changes take effect.
